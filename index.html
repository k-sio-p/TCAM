Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) Calculator

Definition of CAGR

The Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) is a measure used to determine the mean annual growth rate of an investment over a specified period of time. It smooths out the growth rate fluctuations over several years to provide a more accurate picture of the average growth rate.

How to Calculate CAGR

To calculate the CAGR, we use the following formula:

CAGR = [(End Value / Start Value) ^ (1 / Number of Years)] - 1

Where:

  • Start Value is the initial value of the investment.
  • End Value is the final value of the investment.
  • Number of Years is the duration of the investment in years.
